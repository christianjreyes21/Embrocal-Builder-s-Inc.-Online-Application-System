Welcome to your Administrator Account - Summary!

As you can see, there are many fields to be answered and here are some important guidelines on this page to help you:
  • On this page, you can see the summary of the applicants' sorted status and position in the company.
  • Also, you can see here the other tabs to be viewed later on.
  • SEARCH bar with GO button - If you click this, it will let you search the names of the applicants.
  • SORT BY fields with GO button:
  • POSITION dropdown button - If you click this, you will choose a certain position to be seen and sorted.
  • STATUS dropdown button - If you click this, you will choose a certain status to be seen and sorted.
  • This produces a table of names, position and status depending on the sorted categories.
  • On default, all applicants are sorted according to their surnames.
