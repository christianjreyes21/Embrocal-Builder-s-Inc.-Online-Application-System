Here are some instructions to follow in using the system:
Welcome to your Personal Account - Settings!

As you can see, there are many fields to be answered and here are some important guidelines on this page to help you:
  • On this page, you can see three different buttons: CHANGE PASSWORD, CHANGE USERNAME, and DEACTIVATE ACCOUNT.
  • CHANGE PASSWORD button - If you click this, you can change your old password to a new one of your choice. It must be at least 8 characters with 1 number on it.
  • CHANGE USERNAME button - If you click this, you can change your old username to a new one of your choice. It must be at least 6 characters with 1 number on it.
  • DEACTIVATE ACCOUNT - If you click this, there will be a question saying "Are you sure you want to deactivate account." If you choose BACK TO HOME, it means you don't like to remove your account but when you choose CONFIRM then it will ask you to write your password and direct you to the main homepage which means you successfully deactivated your account and it can't be accessed anymore.

Thank you!
