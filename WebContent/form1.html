Here are some instructions to follow in using the system:
Welcome to the On-line Resume of Embrocal Builders Inc. On-line Application System!

As you can see, there are many fields to be answered and here are some important guidelines on this page to help you:
  • UPLOAD IMAGE field - It will let you choose an image file from your computer by clicking the BROWSE button. You will see this attached image together with your complete resume at the last part of the process.
  • DATE OF BIRTH field - It will let you choose your birthday by using a calendar that will pop-up when you click the field itself.
  • You can just type NONE if the field to be answered is really not applicable to you like for example, the NAME OF SPOUSE, HIS/HER OCCUPATION fields etc.
  • NEXT button - If you click this, it will direct you to the next part of the On-line Resume which is the Educational Background.

Thank you!
